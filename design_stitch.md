DESIGN.md — 5S Factory Audit
Style: Industrial, rugged, high-contrast dark theme built for factory-floor readability. Not a soft consumer SaaS look.
Colors:
- Background base: #0D0F12 (near-black)
- Surface/panel: #1A1D21 (dark charcoal)
- Border/divider: #2C3036
- Primary accent (actions, focus): #FFC107 (safety yellow)
- Success/Pass: #00C853 (safety green)
- Danger/Fail: #FF3B30 (safety red)
- Warning/Pending: #FF6D00 (safety orange)
- Text primary: #F5F5F5, text secondary: #9AA0A6
Typography: Bold, heavy sans-serif (Inter or Roboto Condensed, weight 600-800). Min 16px body text, 14px minimum for labels.
Layout rules:
- Minimum touch target 48x48px (glove-friendly)
- Sharp corners, max 4px radius — industrial, not rounded/soft
- Mobile: bottom nav bar. Desktop: left sidebar nav
- Status always shown as a color-coded badge: green=Pass, red=Fail, amber=Pending, gray=N/A

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the 5S Audit System ("the Application").

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in to the Application using Google, we collect the following information from your Google account:

  • Name: Used to identify you as an auditor or manager within the system.
  • Email Address: Used for authentication, account management, and notifications.
  • Profile Picture: Used to display your avatar within the Application interface.

Additionally, we may ask you to provide your Company Email and Department manually upon your first login to associate your account with the correct factory department.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and provide secure access to the Application.
  • To track audit submissions, red tags, and action items created by you.
  • To communicate important updates or notifications related to your tasks.
  • To maintain internal system logs for security and troubleshooting.

3. Data Storage and Security

The Application stores data securely using Google Sheets as a backend database. Access to this data is strictly limited to authorized administrators and managers of your organization. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Data Retention

Your personal information is retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Application's services. If your access is revoked by an administrator, your personal data may still be retained in historical audit logs for compliance and reporting purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, or request that your information be corrected or deleted. Please contact your system administrator to exercise these rights.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

7. Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact your company's IT or EHS department administrator.
